FAQs

Questions we get asked a lot.

Straight answers, no jargon.

How is this different from SEO?
SEO gets you ranked in Google. GEO gets you cited in AI. When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity a question in your category, we make sure your brand is the one they mention — not a competitor. Different channels, different infrastructure, different results.
How long before we see results?
Most clients see measurable AI citation improvements within 60–90 days. We set a clear baseline in week one so progress is trackable from day one, not just a promise at the end of a long retainer.
Do we need to rebuild our whole website?
No. GEO works with your existing site. We add structured data layers and content architecture on top of what you already have. In most cases, your design stays completely untouched.
What if we already have an SEO agency?
Most SEO agencies optimise for Google's ranking algorithm. Very few are building for AI answer engines — which is where purchasing decisions are increasingly being made. We complement your existing SEO, we don't replace it.
Which AI platforms do you optimise for?
We track and optimise for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Gemini, Claude, and Microsoft Copilot. These currently account for the vast majority of AI-driven search and purchasing queries in B2B.
How do you measure success?
We measure Generative Share of Voice — how often and how prominently your brand appears in AI-generated answers for your target queries. You receive a clear monthly report showing citation frequency, sentiment, and competitive position.
